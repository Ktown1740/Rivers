Our Mission

Rivers Shelter Our Mission and Vision The Homeless Project, in partnership with the city of Florida City, is a transformative initiative with a singular, powerful goal: to end homelessness at its root and restore human dignity.

Our mission is not limited to offering temporary shelter; instead, we envision a future where individuals once experiencing homelessness are empowered to regain independence, achieve homeownership, and reenter society as fully self-sufficient citizens. We believe in building not just homes, but lives, careers, and futures.

We see homelessness not just as a lack of shelter, but as a breakdown of support systems, opportunity, and hope. Therefore, our approach is holistic—addressing mental, physical, emotional, and spiritual needs in an environment that reflects care, luxury, and respect.

About Rivers Shelter

Rivers Shelter is not a typical homeless shelter. It is an architectural and social sanctuary designed with the intention of radically reimagining what transitional housing can be. Inspired by elegance and empowerment, the shelter is described as a “classy hotel” or even a “two-story castle,” breaking the conventional mold and erasing the stigma often associated with shelters.

Aesthetic Vision The estate will be gated and beautifully landscaped, with a layout reminiscent of White House-style grounds, suggesting order, dignity, and grandeur. The chosen color palette—royal blue, navy, gold, and platinum—invokes a sense of nobility, creating an environment that uplifts rather than diminishes.

Purpose and Goals

Our primary purpose is to get people off the street and end homelessness in Florida City. Beyond immediate shelter, we aim to:

  • Help residents with resume building and obtaining licenses (e.g., real estate broker's license).
  • Provide financial services and guidance towards best jobs.
  • Offer recreational facilities like a game room and computer room.
  • Provide a chapel for spiritual support.
